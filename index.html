About

Where People Meet Music

PLAN-G (플랜지) is a music label dedicated to discovering authentic artists and maximizing their potential. Home of Gaho (가호), a talented Korean singer-songwriter.

We believe in the power of original artistry and innovative sound. We build deep connections between artists and fans, preserve the true essence of music, and open new possibilities.

From music production to global distribution, PLAN-G stands alongside artists throughout their creative journey, becoming a steadfast partner to help their voices reach the world.
